obj28=<b>Shields</b><br><br>Shields in India were made out of all sorts of materials including cane, wood, buffalo, elephant and rhino hides, turtle shell, steel, silk and even paper pulp.<br><br>Those made in the Mughal court were sometimes decorated with gold and enamel, particularly from the reign of Shah Jahan. However, whatever the material used, the purpose was the same - to protect the wearer from arrows, bullets and axe blows.
