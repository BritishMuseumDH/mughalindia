obj20=<b>The katar and Hinduism</b><br><br>In Indian culture and especially for Hindus, weapons such as this katar dagger, were much more than simply tools of war. Hindu gods were associated with particular weapons and would give them personal names and religious significance.<br><br>The katar for example, is said to come from 'Yama' the god of death. Indeed, weapons were so important that they were sometimes worshipped as gods in themselves.
