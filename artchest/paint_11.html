Finishing Off

When the artist is satisfied with the end result, the whole painting is burnished to a jewel-like sheen that brings out all the colour and brilliance of the artwork.

Extra
The entire Mughal painting process from start to finish would take many hours, many different skills and often more than one artist. A hierarchy existed within the court ateliers - those at the bottom of the scale would do the menial tasks such as grinding pigments and mixing paints. More established artists would design original compositions and do the more delicate, detailed paintwork.
