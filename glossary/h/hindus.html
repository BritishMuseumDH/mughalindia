Hindus / Hindu

A follower of Hinduism.

Hindu beliefs can take on many different forms. Hinduism is a social as well as a religious tradition which began in India, and has developed over thousands of years.

There is no one sacred text for Hindus, but the Bhagavadgita is revered, as are the gods Vishnu (Krishna) and Siva.

Hindu society is structured around a caste system, the highest caste being the Brahman who are the priestly caste.
