Ramayana

The title Ramayana means 'Romance of Rama.' The Ramayana is a Hindu epic poem, describing the royal birth of Rama who famously went on to bend Shiva's bow.

Rama marries Sita, the daughter of King Janaka, and between them they embody the ideal man and woman.

The other famous Hindu epic poem is the Mahabharata. Both were written in around 300 BC.
