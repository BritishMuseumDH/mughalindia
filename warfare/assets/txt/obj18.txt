obj18=<b>War elephants</b><br><br>You can see goads like this one in many Mughal war paintings.<br><br>Elephants played a very important role in Mughal Indian warfare. They were used not only for transport, but as mobile cranes. Elephants could move materials of all kinds, form living bridges over which armies could cross shallow rivers, or push down the gates of besieged fortresses.<br><br>They were used in combat in India as early as the 6th century and there are some accounts of them even being trained to carry lethal swords in their trunks!<BR><BR>Elephants had a terrifying effect on men and horses unfamiliar with them. However, these huge beasts could easily be defeated by well-trained troops, or panicked into trampling down either side at random.
